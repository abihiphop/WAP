In my country, it is natural for every child to contribute its own part on supporting the family especially if the economy of the family is agriculture based. Since my parents own small land area in a small village, I used to help them by taking care of my younger brother and sometimes look after our five to ten sheep as a shepherd in case my older siblings are assigned to other task.

About Abrha Gebreslassie Berhe

I am a 30 years old Abrha Gebreslassie born in Wukro city found in Tigray region (Northern Ethiopia) where the marvelous rock hewn churches are its touristic icon.Tigray is the land of the ancient Axum civilization and its people are symbolic on solidarity to thier unity which makes me proud of. In Ethiopia, it is natural for every child to contribute its own part on supporting the family especially if the economy of the family is agriculture based. Since my parents own small land area in a small village outside Wukro, I used to help them by taking care of my younger brother and sometimes look after our five to ten sheep as a shepherd in case my older siblings are assigned to other task. My parents are recognized by the society as best parents rising disciplined children. In fact every member of my family obeys what the society says it is right to do such as respecting our elders, helping someone in need, refraining from speaking odd words, doing good at education etc.
